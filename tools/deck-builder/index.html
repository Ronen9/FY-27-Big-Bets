Deck Builder
for FY27 Big Bets.

Pick three accounts yourself, or let the magic picker recommend the best three for a given product. Then hit submit and AI builds the deck for you.

Step 1 · Load your MSX export
No data loaded. Drop in your MSX pipeline export or the ME5 IL Whitespace workbook (CSV or XLSX) — the picker auto-detects each file. You can drop both at once to enrich the AI prompt with seat counts and ME5 potential revenue.
Expected columns (either format works)

MSX pipeline export — headers like Account Name (English) or CRM Account Name (required), TPID, Qualified Pipeline, Non-Qualified Pipeline, Primary Sales Play, Primary Competitor, Compete Threat Level, Forecast Recommendation, Summary Segment, ATU, AM, MSX. One row per opportunity. Extra columns are ignored.

ME5 IL Whitespace workbook — we auto-find the right sheet and header row. We look for MSSales Account Name (required), Sub Segment, OnPrem Seats, M365 E3 Seats, M365 E5 Seats, M365 E5 Security Seats, M365 E5 Compliance Seats, IW PCIB, FY26 Flip or GFA, FY27 Whitespace Seats, ME5 full potential revenue ($), Comments. Account names are matched to MSX accounts case-insensitively.

Pick 1–3 accounts.

Choose 1, 2, or 3 accounts from the FY27 open pipeline. Submit becomes active as soon as your first account is picked (each pick must be unique).

🔒 Load your MSX export to enable manual picking.

Let me find the best three.

Pick a product. The picker scores every account in the pipeline by industry fit, deal momentum, white-space, and competitive signal — then explains its top three.

Scoring = industry fit (0–40) + log-pipeline (0–30) + active opportunities (0–15) + compete signal (0–15). Deterministic, no random.

🔒 Load your MSX export to enable the magic picker.

Best 3 whitespace fits for a product.

No MSX pipeline needed. Pick a product — the LLM reads every whitespace account (install base, ME5 headroom, sub-segment) plus recent news on each one (via Tavily), then picks the 3 with the strongest fit and explains why.

Smart picker runs server-side: top ~12 candidates by ME5 potential → Tavily news search per account → AOAI gpt-4o picks the final 3 with reasons + evidence links.

⚠️ Smart picker needs the Python server. Run python tools/deck-builder/server.py and reload — file:// mode can only build the prompt, not run the picker.

🔒 Load the ME5 IL Whitespace workbook to enable this mode.
